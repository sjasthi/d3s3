# V22 — Patient Portal: Resources

**Phase:** 6 — Patient Portal  
**Audience:** Patients  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** V19  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** Has watched V19 (Getting Started)

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** Portal test patient (seed data — patient who has had at least one asset sent to them)
- **Start screen:** Patient portal dashboard — ideally with an unread resources badge visible
- **Test data needed:** At least one asset sent to this patient (from seed data or sent manually in V13 demo). At least one public asset in the library.
- **Note:** The dashboard shows a banner or badge for new/unread resources. Show this first — it's the entry point most patients will use.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:15] Opening
> [On screen: portal dashboard — resources tile or banner with unread count visible]

"The Resources section of your portal is where the clinic can send you educational materials, forms, and other documents relevant to your care. Let me show you how to find and read them."

---

### [0:15–0:40] Finding new resources
> [Action: Point to the unread badge or Resources tile on the dashboard]

"If the clinic has sent you something new, you'll see a notification here on the dashboard — a number showing how many unread items are waiting for you."

> [Action: Click the Resources tile or the nav link to open the Resources page]

> [On screen: portal/resources.php]

---

### [0:40–1:10] Sent resources — from your care team
> [On screen: top section of resources page showing "Sent to You" items]

"The top section shows resources the clinic has sent specifically to you. These are highlighted as new until you open them."

> [Action: Click on an unread resource — e.g., "Blood Pressure Self-Monitoring Guide"]

> [On screen: resource opens or downloads]

"Clicking a resource opens or downloads the file. Once you've opened it, it's marked as read and the notification badge clears."

> [Action: Go back to the resources page — show the item is now marked as read (no unread badge)]

---

### [1:10–1:40] Public library
> [On screen: lower section of resources page — public library grouped by type]

"Below the sent items, you'll see the clinic's public resource library. These are materials the clinic has made available to all patients — general health guides, forms, audio recordings, and more."

> [Action: Scroll through the grouped library. Point to the type groupings — Documents, Videos, etc.]

"You don't need to do anything special to access these — they're available any time you're logged into the portal."

---

### [1:40–2:00] Closing
"Resources are the clinic's way of continuing your care between visits — useful guides, follow-up instructions, or forms you might need to fill out before your next appointment. Check back regularly, especially after a visit."
