# V09 — Tasks: Personal To-Do List

**Phase:** 3 — Communication & Support  
**Audience:** All staff roles  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** V01  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** Basic navigation familiarity

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** Any staff account. Then briefly switch to an ADMIN account to show the admin "all tasks" view.
- **Start screen:** `tasks.php`
- **Test data needed:** A few existing tasks in various states (open, completed) to show a realistic list. If none exist, create them live.
- **Note:** Keep this video light and fast — Tasks is a simple feature. The main things to show are: create, complete, reopen, and admin overview.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:10] Opening
> [On screen: tasks.php]

"The Tasks page is your personal to-do list inside CareSystem. It's useful for tracking follow-up actions, reminders, and anything else you need to keep on top of during your shift."

---

### [0:10–0:35] Creating a task
> [Action: Click "New Task" or "Add Task" button]

"Click New Task to create one. Give it a title — something short and clear. You can add a description for more detail and set a due date if there's a deadline."

> [Action: Fill in Title: "Follow up — Priya Sharma lab results", Description: "Check CBC results and notify Dr. Reddy", Due Date: tomorrow's date]

> [Action: Click Save / Create]

> [On screen: task appears in the list]

---

### [0:35–0:55] Completing a task
> [Action: Find a task in the list. Click the checkbox or "Mark Complete" button.]

"When you've done something, mark it complete by clicking the checkbox. The task moves to the completed section."

> [On screen: task moves to completed state, styled differently (greyed out or checked)]

---

### [0:55–1:15] Reopening a task
> [Action: Find a completed task. Click "Reopen" button.]

"If something comes back up — a task you thought was done but isn't — you can reopen it. Click Reopen and it returns to your active list."

---

### [1:15–1:40] Admin view — all tasks
> [Action: If logged in as ADMIN, point to or show the admin's view that includes all staff tasks]

"Administrators can see tasks across all staff members — not just their own. This makes it easy to check on follow-up items or manage workload across the team. Regular staff only see their own tasks."

---

### [1:40–2:00] Closing
"Tasks are simple but effective for staying organized during a busy shift. The open task count is also shown on your dashboard and in the quick-action strip, so you can always see at a glance what's waiting."
