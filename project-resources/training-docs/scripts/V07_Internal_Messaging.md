# V08 — Internal Staff Messaging

**Phase:** 3 — Communication & Support  
**Audience:** All staff roles  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** V01  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** Basic navigation familiarity

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** Any staff account (TRIAGE_NURSE works well)
- **Start screen:** `dashboard.php` or `messages.php`
- **Test data needed:** At least one other active staff account to message. Ideally have an existing message thread so the inbox isn't empty.
- **Note:** This is the internal staff-to-staff messaging system — completely separate from the patient portal messages (covered in V23). Make that distinction clearly.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:15] Opening
> [On screen: dashboard.php — show the unread messages badge on the sidebar or top nav]

"CareSystem includes an internal messaging system for staff communication. This is separate from patient portal messages — it's for staff-to-staff notes, quick questions, and coordination."

---

### [0:15–0:35] Accessing messages
> [Action: Click the unread badge or click Messages in the sidebar. Navigate to messages.php.]

> [On screen: messages.php — inbox view]

"Clicking Messages opens your inbox. Unread messages are highlighted. The sidebar also shows a red badge with the unread count so you always know when something needs your attention."

---

### [0:35–1:05] Composing a message
> [Action: Click "Compose" or "New Message" button]

> [On screen: compose form]

"Click Compose to write a new message. Start typing the recipient's name — the field auto-searches active staff members. Select the person you want to reach."

> [Action: Type a staff member's name, select them from the suggestion list]

"Add a subject line and your message."

> [Action: Type Subject: "Patient query — Priya Sharma", Body: "Could you confirm which doctor is assigned to today's intake for this patient? Thanks."]

> [Action: Click Send]

---

### [1:05–1:30] Inbox and reading messages
> [On screen: inbox with a received message]

"Received messages appear in your inbox. Click any message to read it. The full conversation thread is shown — you can reply directly from the message view."

> [Action: Click an existing message to open it. Point to the reply area.]

> [Action: Type a brief reply and click Send]

---

### [1:30–1:50] Sent messages
> [Action: Click the Sent tab or link]

"The Sent tab shows everything you've sent, so you can track conversations and confirm your messages were delivered."

---

### [1:50–2:00] Closing
"That's internal messaging in a nutshell — quick, simple, and always accessible from the sidebar. Remember, this is for staff-to-staff communication only. Communicating with patients through the portal is covered in a separate video."
