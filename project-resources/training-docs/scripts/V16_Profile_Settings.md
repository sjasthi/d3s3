# V17 — Your Profile & Settings

**Phase:** 5 — Personal & Role Orientation  
**Audience:** All staff roles  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** V01  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** None beyond V01

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** Any staff account
- **Start screen:** `dashboard.php`
- **Test data needed:** None
- **Note:** Keep this light and fast. Profile and Settings are simple pages. The gear icon (display settings panel) is available on every page — show it from the dashboard rather than navigating away.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:10] Opening
> [On screen: dashboard.php]

"In this short video I'll show how to update your profile and customize your display settings. These options are available to every staff member."

---

### [0:10–0:40] Profile page
> [Action: Click your name or "Profile" in the sidebar]

> [On screen: profile.php]

"The Profile page lets you update your display name, email address, and password. If your name has changed or you want to update your contact email, this is where to do it."

> [Action: Update the email field with a demo change. Click Save.]

> [On screen: success message]

"Changes save immediately."

> [Action: Scroll to the password change section]

"To change your password, enter your current password and then your new one. This is recommended if you were given a temporary password when your account was created."

---

### [0:40–1:10] Settings page
> [Action: Click Settings in the sidebar]

> [On screen: settings.php]

"The Settings page has a few personalisation options — language, font size, and your preferred theme."

> [Action: Point to the language selector — English / Telugu]

"Language can be switched between English and Telugu. The interface will update immediately."

> [Action: Point to the font size option]

"If you find the default text size hard to read, the Large option increases it across the entire interface."

---

### [1:10–1:40] Display settings panel — gear icon
> [Action: Navigate back to dashboard. Click the gear icon in the top-right navbar.]

> [On screen: slide-down settings panel]

"You don't have to go to Settings to toggle dark mode or change language — the gear icon in the top navigation opens a quick settings panel. This panel is available on every page, so you can switch modes mid-session."

> [Action: Toggle dark mode on. Point out the interface change. Toggle back off.]

"Dark mode and font size preferences are remembered between sessions."

---

### [1:40–2:00] Closing
"That's everything for profile and settings. They're simple but worth knowing — especially the gear icon shortcut, which saves you a trip to the Settings page whenever you want to flip to dark mode."
