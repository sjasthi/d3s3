# V19 — Patient Portal: Getting Started

**Phase:** 6 — Patient Portal  
**Audience:** Patients  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** (None — this is the first portal video)  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** Has received portal login credentials from the clinic

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** Portal test patient (patient ID 1, 2, or 3 from seed data — password: `Portal@1234`)
- **Start screen:** `patient_login.php` — logged out
- **Test data needed:** Active portal account with a username/password. The test email from seed data.
- **Note:** The patient portal is completely separate from the staff system — different login page, different session, different navigation. Make this distinction explicit at the start.
- **Tone:** This video should feel welcoming and simple. The audience is patients, not technical staff.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:15] Opening
> [On screen: patient_login.php]

"Welcome to the CareSystem patient portal. This is your secure online space to view your health information, message your care team, and access resources from the clinic. In this video I'll show you how to log in and get oriented."

---

### [0:15–0:40] Logging in
> [Action: Type the portal email or username in the login field]

"Your clinic will give you a username and a temporary password when your portal account is set up. Enter those here and click Log In."

> [Action: Enter credentials and click Log In]

> [On screen: portal dashboard — patient view]

"You're now in your patient portal. This is separate from any staff logins — if you work at the clinic, you'll have a separate staff account."

---

### [0:40–1:10] Portal dashboard overview
> [On screen: portal/dashboard.php — patient dashboard]

"The dashboard shows you a summary of your care — upcoming appointments, any new lab results, unread messages from your care team, and resources the clinic has sent you."

> [Action: Point to each summary card briefly — appointments, health record, messages, resources]

"Use the navigation bar at the top to move between sections. We'll cover each section in the following videos."

---

### [1:10–1:40] Profile — updating your information
> [Action: Click Profile in the nav bar]

> [On screen: portal/profile.php]

"The Profile page lets you review and update your contact information. Importantly, the allergies field here feeds directly into your clinical record — if you have a new allergy to report, enter it here and your care team will see it on your next visit."

> [Action: Point to the allergies field — type an example: "Sulfonamides (rash)"]

"Always keep your phone number and email current so the clinic can reach you."

> [Action: Click Save]

---

### [1:40–2:00] Closing
"That's the basics — logging in and finding your way around. The next videos in this series cover your Health Record, lab results, messaging your care team, and the resources library."
